Die Philosophen haben die Welt nur verschieden interpretiert; es kömmt drauf an, sie zu verändern. Karl Marx, Thesen über Feuerbach, 1845.

Karl Marx (1818 – 1883) was one of the most influential philosophers and thinkers of the nineteenth century.

Introductions, Biographies and Overviews

The English Wikipedia article Karl Marx and the German Wikipedia article Karl Marx contain a basic bibliography.

  • Jones, Josh: David Harvey’s Course on Marx’s Capital: Volumes 1 & 2 Now Available Free Online, Open Culture, 20.11.2014.
  • Carver, Terrell: The Cambridge Companion to Marx. Cambridge Companions to Philosophy. Cambridge University Press, 1992. (376 pages) ISBN 9780521366946.
  • Carver, Terrell; Farr, James: The Cambridge Companion to The Communist Manifesto. Cambridge Companions to Philosophy. Cambridge University Press, 2015. (310 pages) ISBN 9781107683075.
  • Leopold, David: The Young Karl Marx: German Philosophy, Modern Politics, and Human Flourishing. Ideas in Context. Cambridge University Press, 2009. (340 pages) ISBN 9780521118262.

    • Works

    Marx's works are now in the public domain, so several web sites provide free access to many of his texts. For example, Wikisource (German), Wikisource (English), Project Gutenberg (Project Gutenberg blocks access from Germany as of February 2018), Zeno.org and the Internet Archive. The German Wikipedia article Werkverzeichnis claims to provide a complete list.
    Selected works (links to German Wikipedia articles):
