Ethics or Morality Based on Science

  • Harris, Sam: The Moral Landscape: How Science Can Determine Human Values. Free Press, 2010. ISBN 9781439171226. See also the article The Moral Landscape on Wikipedia.
  • Harris, Sam: Science Can Answer Moral Questions (YouTube, 23 minutes, 2010): talk at TED, February 2010. See also the TED website.
  • Earp, Brian D.: Can science determine morality? Sam Harris criticized (17 February 2016): a 34-minute video in which J. J. Chipchase interviews Oxford-based philosopher Brian D. Earp about Sam Harris's book The Moral Landscape. Topics include the is-ought-problem, utilitarianism, and Harris's conception of science as rational enquiry or secular moral philosophy, the limits of peer review in science journals, and free will. See also the Chipchase's blog post Informal Hour Ep. 2 – Science and Morality (The Moral Landscape) with Brian D. Earp (17 February 2016).
